Dedicated to Closing the Queer Digital Divide.




Digital spaces haven't been built for queer people. Rampant homophobia on social networks, transphobia in LLMs, and the marginalization of queer engineers are all the results of a disregard of queer people in tech. Through the development of queer-positive open-source software, we're fighting the latest front of the broader struggle for queer rights worldwide.


We are the Queer Digital Front.





Our Goals:

Inclusion

Ensuring that digital spaces are accessible and hospitable to queer people

Innovation

Building digital tools to solve issues affecting the queer community

Inspiration

Training and supporting queer engineers, and promoting digital literacy in queer people
