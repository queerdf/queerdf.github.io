Dedicated to Closing the Queer Digital Divide.




Digital spaces haven't been built for queer people. Rampant homophobia on social networks, transphobia in language models, and the marginalization of queer engineers are all the results of a disregard of queer people in tech, itself just one front of the broader struggle for queer rights worldwide.

We are the Queer Digital Front.

Our Goals:

Access

Ensuring that digital spaces are accessible and hospitable to queer people

Aid

Building digital tools to solve issues affecting the queer community

Ability

Training and supporting queer engineers, and promoting digital literacy in queer people
